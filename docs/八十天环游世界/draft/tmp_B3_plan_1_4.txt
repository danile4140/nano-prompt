# 批次 B3_1_4

## 分镜 B3-1

**覆盖原文：**

S1: Inspector Fix was one of the detectives investigating the robbery at the Bank of England.
译：菲克斯探长是调查英格兰银行盗窃案的侦探之一。

S2: In his years as a detective he knew only one thing for certain: all the biggest criminals looked like respectable gentlemen.
译：多年侦探生涯中，他有一件事确信无疑：最大的罪犯往往看起来像受人尊敬的绅士。

S3: The money, the quick departure.
译：钱款、仓促离去。

S4: It all made sense.
译：一切都说得通。

S5: Phileas Fogg was a respectable gentleman, and he, Inspector Fix, wanted to get the reward for catching him.
译：费利亚斯·福格是个体面绅士，而他——菲克斯探长——想要得到抓住他的赏金。

## 分镜 B3-2

**覆盖原文：**

S6: He soon discovered that Phileas Fogg was on the Mongolia, a ship that sailed from Brindisi, in Italy, to Bombay, in India.
译：他很快查明，费利亚斯·福格乘坐的是"蒙古利亚号"，一艘从意大利布林迪西驶往印度孟买的轮船。

S7: Our detective decided to look carefully at all the people getting on and off the Mongolia.
译：我们的侦探决定仔细盯守所有上下"蒙古利亚号"的乘客。

S8: On Wednesday, 9 October, Inspector Fix saw Phileas Fogg and his manservant as they arrived in the Suez Canal.
译：10月9日星期三，菲克斯探长在苏伊士运河看见了费利亚斯·福格和他的仆人抵达。
