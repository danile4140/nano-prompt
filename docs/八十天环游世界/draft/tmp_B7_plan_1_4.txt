# 批次 B7_1_4

## 分镜 B7-1

**覆盖句子：**

Their journey continued across the mountains of Colorado with no other surprises.
译：他们的旅途继续穿越科罗拉多山脉，没有遇到其他意外。

At least for now.
译：至少目前如此。

In three days and three nights, they traveled more than 2,200 kilometres.
译：三天三夜，他们行驶了2200多公里。

---


## 分镜 B7-2

**覆盖句子：**

The passengers were soon familiar with the delays; these only seemed to worry Passepartout.
译：旅客们很快便习惯了延误；似乎只有帕斯帕图为此忧心忡忡。

Phileas Fogg continued to play cards with Mrs Aouda, while Inspector Fix slept like a baby, his head going up and down with the gentle movement of the train.
译：菲利亚斯·福格继续和奥达夫人打牌，而菲克斯探长则睡得像婴儿一样，头随着火车的轻柔颠簸一上一下。

---


## 分镜 B7-3

**覆盖句子：**

Passepartout correctly predicted more problems; he was right; a few days later a group of Sioux Indians attached their train.
译：帕斯帕图正确地预感到会有更多麻烦；他猜对了——几天后，一队苏族印第安人袭击了他们的火车。

---


## 分镜 B7-4

**覆盖句子：**

The Sioux rode their horses along both sides of the train.
译：苏族人骑着马沿火车两侧奔驰。

The passengers heard their battle cries before they arrived.
译：旅客们在苏族人到来之前就已听到他们的战斗呐喊。

Many of them knew what was happening, the horses of the Sioux moved faster than the train and several arrows flew towards the train carriages, until finally the train slowed down.
译：许多人意识到发生了什么——苏族人的马比火车跑得更快，数支箭矢射向车厢，火车终于开始减速。

Some of the passengers prepared to defend themselves.
译：部分旅客开始准备自卫。
