- S3: The money, the quick departure.

- S7: Our detective decided to look carefully at all the people getting on and off the Mongolia.
- S8: On Wednesday, 9 October, Inspector Fix saw Phileas Fogg and his manservant as they arrived in the Suez Canal.

- S9: 'So, there's our thief!' he whispered.
- S10: 'All I need to do now is to tell Scotland Yard and wait for a warrant for his arrest, and then the reward is mine.'

**段落五（Fix 决定接近帕斯帕图）**

- S11: Fix decided to speak to Fogg's manservant.

**段落六（对话第一轮：开场寒暄）**

- S12: 'Egypt is a beautiful country,' began the Inspector.
- S13: 'Yes, that's true, but we are travelling so quickly,' replied Passepartout.

**段落七（对话第二轮：为何如此快？/80天之旅）**

- S14: 'Why are you travelling so quickly?
- S15: Surely you can't see Egypt in only a few days.'
- S16: 'My master wants to travel around the world in eighty days…' he said, looking at the detective's confused face.
- S17: 'I know, it's complete madness.'

**段落八（对话第三轮：Fix 评论主人富有；帕斯帕图告别）**

- S20: But… I really must leave.
- S21: We have a boat to catch.
- S22: Good day, Mr…?'

**段落九（Fix 自我介绍 + 问孟买）**

- S23: 'Fix, my name is Ins…, Mr Fix.
- S24: And I believe that we are possibly going the same way.
- S25: Are you also going to Bombay?'

**段落十（帕斯帕图自我介绍 + 挥手告别）**

- S26: 'Yes, we are.
- S27: Sorry, not to introduce myself.
- S28: My name's Jean Passepartout.
- S29: I'm sure we'll see each other again.'
- S30: Passepartout touched his hat and waved goodbye.

**段落十一（Fix 更加确信福格是窃贼，忧虑）**

- S31: His conversation with Passepartout made Inspector Fix feel even more certain that Phileas Fogg was the thief.
- S32: 'I must stop him,' he thought.
- S33: But how?
- S34: Without the warrant for his arrest it was impossible, and he could escape again.
- S35: He was worried.

**段落十二（福格从容规划行程）**

- S36: Phileas Fogg, on the other hand, was carefully planning his journey.
- S37: He kept detailed notes of the date, the length of each part of the journey, the time and the places they stopped in.

**段落十三（船离苏伊士前往孟买 / 福格打牌）**

- S38: On 10 October, the ship left Suez for the next stop – Bombay.
- S39: The sea was rough, but Phileas Fogg was not worried and soon found people on the ship to play cards with.

**段落十四（抵达孟买，上岸前往车站）**

- S40: On 20 October they arrived in Bombay.
- S41: Phileas Fogg and his manservant left the ship and went to the station to catch the train to Calcutta.

**段落十五（帕斯帕图忧虑赌约）**

- S42: On their way there Passepartout began to think about his master's bet.
- S43: He felt worried.
- S44: Yes, it was true, they were two days early, but anything could still happen.

**段落十六（火车准时出发）**

- S45: The train left Bombay on time.

**段落十七（三天后火车停车，司机宣布铁路未完工）**

- S46: Three days later the train stopped at a small village.
- S47: Passepartout heard the train driver shout, 'Everone must get off.
- S48: The railway line ends here!'
- S49: The railway line from Bombay to Calcutta was not yet finished.
- S50: The passengers had to travel to the next station, Allahabad, on their own.

**段落十八（乘客各自寻找交通工具）**

- S51: People who often travelled between the two towns were quick to find a way to continue their journey.
- S52: Among the different types of transport there were little carts pulled by cow and ponies, and rickshaws pulled by bicycles or the men from the village.

**段落十九（福格购买大象 Kiouni，踏上旅途）**

- S53: Passepartout was worried about how to get to the next station, but Phileas Fogg immediately found a man with an elephant and after a short discussion, the man sold him the elephant for a very high price, and the two travelers were soon on their way to the next station with a guide and the elephant, called Kiouni.

**段落二十（夜晚抵达棕榈树森林，停歇）**

- S54: At about nine o'clock that night our adventurers came to a big forest of palm trees where they had to stop to let Kiouni rest and eat the leaves from the tress.

**段落二十一（丛林中数日宿营至大象突停）**

- S55: For a few days they slept in huts in the middle of the jungle.
- S56: Sometimes they heard the cries of the monkeys and the sound of the tigers.
- S57: Their journey was going well until the elephant suddenly stopped.
