# 批次 B7_25_28

## 分镜 B7-25

**覆盖句子：**

At that moment Inspector Fix returned with a man he was talking to outside the station.
译：就在那一刻，菲克斯探长带着一个他在站外搭话的男子回来了。

'This man says he can take us to the station in Omaha in his sledge,' he said.
译："这位先生说他可以用雪橇把我们送到奥马哈车站，"他说。

'We can take a train to New York from there.'
译："我们可以从那里搭火车去纽约。"

The man had a strange sledge with sails.
译：那人有一架奇特的帆动雪橇。

He explained to them that he often took passengers from one station to another in the winter, when the snow stopped the trains, and that with a good wind behind them, they could go a lot faster than the train.
译：他向他们解释说，冬天积雪阻断列车时，他常常用雪橇载客往返于各车站之间；只要顺风，速度可以比火车快得多。

## 分镜 B7-27

**覆盖句子：**

They all climbed onto the sledge.
译：所有人都爬上了雪橇。

The sledge traveled very quickly across the icy, flat lands of the central states.
译：雪橇飞快地穿越中部各州冰封的平坦大地。

The passengers were very cold, and with an icy wind blowing in their ears they did not speak for most of the journey.
译：旅客们冻得厉害，冰冷的寒风刮过耳边，大部分路程他们都默不作声。

**覆盖句子：**

They were in Omaha in less than five hours.
译：不到五小时，他们便抵达了奥马哈。

When they arrived, they thanked the man and Phileas Fogg paid him well.
译：到达后，他们向那人道谢，菲利亚斯·福格给了他丰厚的报酬。
